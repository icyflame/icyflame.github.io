Indian Traffic Signs

IIITDM, Kancheepuram

Summer 2014

As an Image Processing Intern at IIITDM, Kancheepuram, I worked on "Detection and Classification of Indian Traffic signs". In a team of 2, we built a Matlab Module, which would take images (from a camera mounted on the vehicle) as input and classify them, and give meaningful information, gained from the traffic sign detected (if any) in the image, to the driver.
